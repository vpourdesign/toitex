Toitex inc. was born from a simple conviction: every property owner deserves a roof installed with the same care as if it were our own. Based in Boisbriand, in the heart of Montreal's North Shore, our company brings together a team of passionate roofers trained in the latest techniques and committed to total quality. Whether for a single-family home or a multi-storey commercial building, we bring the same rigor to every project.

Chaque matériau que nous utilisons est sélectionné pour sa durabilité et sa performance face aux conditions climatiques exigeantes du Québec — des vagues de chaleur estivales aux tempêtes de verglas hivernales. Our expertise spans the full roofing spectrum: elastomeric membrane installation for flat roofs, asphalt shingle applications, emergency repairs, preventive inspections, thermal insulation, and winter snow removal. Every material we use is selected for its durability and performance against Quebec's demanding climate — from summer heat waves to winter ice storms.
